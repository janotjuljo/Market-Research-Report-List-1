<p><strong>The Steam Expanders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-hermetic Steam Scroll Expander</li><li>Steam Screw Expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam expanders market comprises various types, notably semi-hermetic steam scroll expanders and steam screw expanders. Semi-hermetic steam scroll expanders feature a compact design, providing efficient energy conversion with minimal leakage, making them suitable for low to medium power applications. On the other hand, steam screw expanders use a helical design to handle larger capacities and are ideal for high-power systems, ensuring reliability and robust performance in industrial applications, including waste heat recovery and combined heat and power systems.</p></p>
