<p><p>Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) is a network technology that examines data packets transmitted over networks, allowing for enhanced data analysis, security, and traffic management. The DPI market can be categorized into two types: Standalone DPI and Integrated DPI. Standalone DPI solutions function independently, focusing solely on packet inspection, while Integrated DPI solutions are embedded within broader networking devices, such as firewalls or routers, providing seamless functionality and enhanced network performance by combining inspection capabilities with other features.</p></p>
