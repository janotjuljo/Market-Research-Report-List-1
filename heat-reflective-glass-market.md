<p><strong>The Heat Reflective Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft-coat Glass</li><li>Hard-coat Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat reflective glass market comprises primarily two types: soft-coat glass and hard-coat glass. Soft-coat glass features a low-emissivity coating applied in a controlled environment, offering superior thermal performance and enhancing energy efficiency by reflecting infrared radiation. In contrast, hard-coat glass has a durable ceramic coating applied during manufacturing, providing a more robust surface suitable for exterior applications, though it generally has lesser thermal performance compared to soft-coat. Both types are crucial for energy-efficient building designs.</p></p>
