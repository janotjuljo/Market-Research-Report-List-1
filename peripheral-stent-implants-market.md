<p><ul><li>Bare Metal Stents</li><li>Covered Stents</li><li>Drug Eluting Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral stent implants market encompasses three main types: Bare Metal Stents (BMS), Covered Stents, and Drug-Eluting Stents (DES). Bare Metal Stents are simple metallic structures that provide support to narrowed arteries. Covered Stents feature a membrane that prevents tissue in-growth, useful in specific clinical situations. Drug-Eluting Stents are coated with medication that gradually releases to inhibit restenosis, enhancing long-term patency rates. Each type is designed to address varying medical needs in peripheral artery diseases.</p></p>
