<p><ul><li>Air Spring Suspension Fork</li><li>Coil Spring Suspension Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike fork market primarily features two types of suspension systems: air spring forks and coil spring forks. Air spring suspension forks are lightweight and adjustable, offering customizable damping and responsiveness based on rider preference and terrain. They utilize compressed air to provide support, improving performance on diverse trails. Conversely, coil spring suspension forks use metal coils for a more consistent feel and reliability. They tend to be heavier but can deliver excellent durability and performance in rugged conditions.</p></p>
