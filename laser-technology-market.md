<p><strong>The Laser Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Laser</li><li>Liquid Laser</li><li>Gas Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser technology market can be categorized into three main types: solid lasers, liquid lasers, and gas lasers. Solid lasers utilize solid-state gain media, offering high efficiency and durability, suitable for industrial applications. Liquid lasers, often using dye solutions, provide tunability and broader wavelength ranges, making them ideal for biomedical and research applications. Gas lasers, which use gases like helium-neon or carbon dioxide as the medium, are known for their precision and are commonly used in cutting, welding, and medical procedures.</p></p>
