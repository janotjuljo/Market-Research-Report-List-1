<p><p>The reusable resuscitator market comprises self-inflating and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are compact devices that automatically refill with air when released, making them user-friendly and portable, suitable for emergency settings. Flow-inflating resuscitators, on the other hand, require a gas source to inflate and are typically used in controlled environments like hospitals, offering precise ventilation. Both types are essential in providing immediate respiratory support during emergencies, catering to different clinical needs and settings.</p></p>
