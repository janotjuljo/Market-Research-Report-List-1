<p><strong>The Printed Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Glued Labels</li><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Shrink Sleeves</li><li>Stretch Sleeves</li><li>Pre-Gummed</li><li>In-Mould Labels</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printed labels market comprises various types, each serving unique applications. Wet glued labels are affixed using adhesives and are commonly used on glass and metal containers. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere without any additional glue, making them versatile for different surfaces. Shrink sleeves conform to the shape of the product when heated, providing full-surface decoration. Stretch sleeves expand to fit containers, while pre-gummed labels come with adhesive on one side for easy application. In-mould labels are integrated during manufacturing for durability, with the "others" category encompassing niche or specialized label types.</p></p>
