<p><ul><li>Metal</li><li>Ceramic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Force sensing capacitors (FSCs) are categorized primarily into metal and ceramic types based on their construction materials. Metal force sensing capacitors utilize conductive materials that provide robustness and high sensitivity, making them suitable for demanding applications in industrial settings. In contrast, ceramic force sensing capacitors are based on dielectric materials, offering excellent stability and environmental resistance. Both types find applications in touch interfaces, robotics, and automotive systems, with each material type catering to specific performance requirements and environments.</p></p>
