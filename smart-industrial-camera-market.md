<p><p>The smart industrial camera market encompasses various types, primarily area scan and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture entire images in a single shot, making them suitable for applications requiring high-resolution images of stationary objects across broad fields. Conversely, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, ideal for inspecting moving objects in continuous processes, such as in manufacturing. Both types enhance automation and quality control in industries by providing real-time visual data for analysis and decision-making.</p></p>
