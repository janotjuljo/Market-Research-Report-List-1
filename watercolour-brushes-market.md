<p><strong>The Watercolour Brushes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Hair Brushes</li><li>Synthetic Hair Brushes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The watercolour brushes market is primarily divided into two types: natural hair brushes and synthetic hair brushes. Natural hair brushes, made from animal fibers such as sable or squirrel, offer superior pigment retention and smooth application. They are often favored by professional artists for their ability to create fine details and smooth washes. In contrast, synthetic hair brushes are made from man-made fibers, providing durability and easy maintenance. They are versatile, more affordable, and increasingly replicating the performance of natural brushes, catering to hobbyists and beginners.</p></p>
