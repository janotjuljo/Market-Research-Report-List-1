<p><p>The articulated dump truck tire market consists of three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering good traction and shock absorption, making them suitable for diverse terrains. Solid tires are robust and puncture-resistant, ideal for harsh environments where durability is essential. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, providing excellent wear resistance and cushioning, making them versatile for both on-road and off-road applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs in construction and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
