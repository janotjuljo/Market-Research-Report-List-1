<p><p>Paddy dryer markets can be categorized into stationary and mobile types. Stationary paddy dryers are fixed installations at processing facilities, designed for high-capacity drying and efficiency. They cater to large-scale operations, ensuring uniform drying and minimal labor input. In contrast, mobile paddy dryers are transportable units that can be relocated to different farming sites. They provide flexibility for smaller producers, allowing on-site drying and minimizing post-harvest losses. Both types serve distinct needs within the agricultural drying process.</p></p>
