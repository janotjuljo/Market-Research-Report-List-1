<p><strong>The Automotive Part Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive part die casting market comprises several techniques, including pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, and semi-solid die casting. </p><p>- **Pressure die casting** involves forcing molten metal into a mold at high pressure, offering precision and smooth finishes.</p><p>- **Vacuum die casting** minimizes air entrapment for improved integrity and surface quality. </p><p>- **Squeeze die casting** employs compressive force to enhance material density and strength. </p><p>- **Semi-solid die casting** utilizes a thixotropic alloy for better flow and lower defects, enhancing performance in automotive applications.</p></p>
