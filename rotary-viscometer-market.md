<p><strong>The Rotary Viscometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylinder Type</li><li>Cone-Plate Type</li><li>Parallel-Plate Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary viscometer market comprises three primary types: cylinder type, cone-plate type, and parallel-plate type. Cylinder-type viscometers measure viscosity by rotating a cylinder within a fluid. Cone-plate viscometers use a conical geometry to provide precise measurements with minimal fluid volume, ideal for low-viscosity samples. Parallel-plate viscometers consist of two flat plates, allowing for accurate viscosity measurement across a wide range of sample types. Each type caters to specific applications, enhancing viscosity measurement in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and polymers.</p></p>
