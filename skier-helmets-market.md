<p><p>The skier helmets market primarily features two material types: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and PC (Polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their durability and impact resistance, making them suitable for recreational skiing. They are generally lighter and offer good protection at a more affordable price. In contrast, PC helmets provide superior strength and resistance to temperature fluctuations, making them highly effective for professional skiers. Both materials prioritize safety, comfort, and performance, catering to different segments of the skiing community.</p></p>
