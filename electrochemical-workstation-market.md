<p><p>The electrochemical workstation market is segmented into single-channel and multichannel types. Single-channel workstations focus on one electrochemical measurement at a time, making them ideal for specific applications requiring detailed analysis, such as fundamental research. In contrast, multichannel workstations enable simultaneous testing of multiple samples, enhancing throughput and efficiency for applications like screening and quality control. Both types cater to diverse research needs, influencing user choice based on sample volume, complexity, and desired measurement precision.</p></p>
