<p><p>The thermoforming packaging market includes several types, primarily blister packs, clamshells, vacuum packs, and skin packs. Clamshells feature a hinged design for easy opening and visibility, commonly used for retail products. Vacuum packs remove air to extend shelf life, suitable for perishable foods. Skin packs adhere products to a backing card, providing protection and visibility, often used in food and consumer electronics.</p></p>
