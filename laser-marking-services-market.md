<p><p>Laser marking services encompass various technologies, primarily classified into CO2, YAG, and others. CO2 lasers generate high-quality markings on non-metallic materials like plastics and wood, known for their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) lasers are ideal for marking metals and ceramics, offering precision and durability. Other technologies may include fiber lasers, which provide versatility for both metals and plastics, along with UV lasers effective for sensitive materials. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as manufacturing, packaging, and automotive.</p></p>
