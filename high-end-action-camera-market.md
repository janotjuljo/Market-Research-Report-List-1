<p><p>The high-end action camera market is primarily divided into two types: box type cameras and bullet type cameras. Box type cameras are compact, often rectangular designs that offer versatile mounting options, superior image quality, and advanced features, making them ideal for various activities such as sports and travel. Bullet type cameras, on the other hand, are elongated and streamlined, designed for specific applications like motorcycling or cycling. These cameras are typically lightweight and can be easily attached to helmets or bikes, enhancing portability and ease of use.</p></p>
