<p><p>The medical ultrasonic cleaners market can be categorized into two main types: single tank type and multi-tank type. Single tank type units consist of one chamber for cleaning instruments, making them suitable for smaller facilities needing efficient, space-saving solutions. In contrast, multi-tank type systems feature several interconnected tanks, allowing for sequential cleaning, rinsing, and drying processes. This versatility makes them ideal for larger healthcare settings that require more extensive cleaning capabilities and higher throughput for sterilizing multiple instruments simultaneously.</p></p>
