<p><ul><li>Variable Attenuator</li><li>Fixed Attenuator</li><li>Programmable Attenuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable attenuator market is segmented into three main types: variable attenuators, fixed attenuators, and programmable attenuators. Variable attenuators allow users to adjust signal strength dynamically, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility. Fixed attenuators provide a consistent reduction in signal strength and are often used in specific setups where fixed attenuation is needed. Programmable attenuators offer advanced control, enabling users to set varying levels of attenuation through digital interfaces, enhancing precision in signal management for complex systems.</p></p>
