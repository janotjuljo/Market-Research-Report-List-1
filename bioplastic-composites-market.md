<p><p>The bioplastic composites market is categorized into natural and synthetic polymer types. Natural polymer composites are derived from renewable sources like starch, cellulose, and proteins, offering biodegradability and sustainability. These are suitable for applications in packaging and agricultural films. Synthetic polymer composites, on the other hand, involve petroleum-based polymers blended with natural fillers. They provide enhanced mechanical properties and durability, making them ideal for automotive and construction industries. Both types contribute to reducing reliance on fossil fuels and promoting eco-friendly solutions.</p></p>
