<p><p>The whey protein market is segmented into several types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), Hydrolyzed Whey Protein, and Others. WPC typically contains 70-80% protein and retains some fats and carbohydrates, making it popular for its nutritional benefits. WPI is more refined, boasting 90% protein with minimal fats and lactose, ideal for those seeking higher protein content. Hydrolyzed whey undergoes partial hydrolysis for quick absorption, often favored in medical or sports nutrition. "Others" may include blends or specialty formulations targeting specific health needs.</p></p>
