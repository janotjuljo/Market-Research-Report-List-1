<p><strong>The Garment Interlining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusible interlining</li><li>Non Fuse Interlining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The garment interlining market is primarily divided into two types: fusible interlining and non-fusible interlining. Fusible interlining is bonded to the fabric using heat and pressure, providing structure and support while allowing for easier application. Non-fusible interlining, on the other hand, is sewn or tacked into place, offering versatility for various fabric types and applications without the need for adhesives. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the durability, fit, and overall quality of garments.</p></p>
