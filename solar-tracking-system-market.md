<p><strong>The Solar Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis Solar Tracking System</li><li>Dual Axis Solar Tracking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar tracking system market comprises two main types: single-axis and dual-axis solar tracking systems. Single-axis trackers rotate on one axis, following the sun's movement from east to west, enhancing energy capture throughout the day. They are cost-effective and widely used in large solar farms. Dual-axis trackers, on the other hand, adjust on both horizontal and vertical axes, maximizing solar exposure during different seasons. While more complex and expensive, they significantly boost energy efficiency and are ideal for locations with variable sunlight.</p></p>
