<p><ul><li>Frontal Airbag ECU</li><li>Curtain Airbag ECU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive airbag ECU market consists of various types, including frontal airbag ECU and curtain airbag ECU. Frontal airbag ECU is designed to deploy airbags in the event of a head-on collision, protecting the occupants from frontal impacts. In contrast, curtain airbag ECU operates to deploy side airbags that protect passengers' heads during side-impact crashes. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by managing airbag deployment in response to specific crash dynamics, thus improving occupant protection.</p></p>
