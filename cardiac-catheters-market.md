<p><strong>The Cardiac Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Guiding Catheters</li><li>Pulmonary Artery Catheters</li><li>PTCA Balloon Catheters</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac catheters market comprises various types designed for specific diagnostic and therapeutic purposes. Angiography catheters facilitate imaging of blood vessels. Guiding catheters assist in maneuvering other devices during procedures. Pulmonary artery catheters monitor heart function and pressure. PTCA balloon catheters are used for angioplasty, widening narrowed arteries. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide real-time imaging of vessel walls. Electrophysiology catheters assess electrical activity in the heart, aiding in the diagnosis of arrhythmias. Together, these catheters enhance cardiovascular care and treatment.</p></p>
