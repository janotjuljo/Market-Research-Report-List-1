<p><strong>The Carbon Nanotube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT)</li><li>Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT)</li><li>Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon nanotube market is primarily categorized into three types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT), and Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT). SWNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical shape, offering exceptional electrical and thermal properties. MWNTs contain multiple layers, enhancing structural strength and conductivity. DWNTs serve as an intermediate option, featuring two concentric layers. Each type has unique applications in electronics, materials science, and nanotechnology, driving demand across various industries.</p></p>
